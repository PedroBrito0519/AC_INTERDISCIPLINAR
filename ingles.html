How the TECHNOLOGY works
TECCARGA is a systematic AI that optimizes the delivery of goods with drones and sensors, ensuring efficiency and sustainability. In addition to ensuring that products arrive in good condition, it has three main functions within the company:

01

Electric Drones

The electric drones managed by TECCARGA emit far fewer pollutants than traditional vehicles. This helps improve air quality, making cities healthier and more pleasant for all their inhabitants.

02

Decreased Traffic

With the delivery of goods carried out by drones, TECCARGA reduces the number of trucks and vans on the streets, decreasing traffic and resulting in less pollution from gases and less noise pollution, creating a quieter urban environment.

03

Automated Monitoring of Goods Conditions

TECCARGA constantly monitors the conditions of goods, such as temperature and humidity, ensuring that sensitive products arrive to the consumer in good condition, thus reducing waste.

High product prices: Improvements in logistics infrastructure, such as more efficient roads or optimized logistics systems, can reduce logistics costs and thus prices for consumers.
